Gioco Timer

How to Play

A fast-paced verbal challenge for players:

2-Player Mode:

  • Each player has their own timer.
  • When it's your turn, your timer counts down.
  • Press your button when you finish your turn to switch to the next player.
  • The game ends when a player's timer reaches zero.

3-Player Mode:

  • When a player presses their button:
  • - Their timer starts increasing (gaining time)
  • - The other two players' timers start decreasing
  • - Any player with a decreasing timer can press next
  • Players are eliminated when their timer reaches zero
  • Last player remaining wins!
Pro tip: Tap your button when you say a word to gain time or switch turns!
